# MOSAIC: A **M**odular **O**pen **S**ystem for **A**daptive **I**nteraction and **C**o-design
[![License: GPL-3.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-GPLv3-blue)](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.en.html)


**MOSAIC** is a modular social robot platform for child-centered co-design with three different embodiments: a humanoid, an abstract, and a zoomorphic appearance. The embodiment is easily adaptable or extendable as it is 3D-printed and snaps on via a magnetic mechanism.

**MOSAIC** is designed as a dynamically evolving and openly accessible co-design toolkit for the community. We highly encourage anyone who is interested to use it as such, share their experiences, contribute to its further development, and thus help spread the practice of co-designing robots with their intended users.
